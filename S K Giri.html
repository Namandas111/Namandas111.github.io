02 / OBJECTIVE
About
SCALE N.T.S.

I'm an architect who treats every brief — residential, commercial, or municipal — as a problem to be drawn through, not around. My work spans the full span of a project's documentation: concept sketches, block models, construction-ready drafting, and the 3D renders that let a client walk through a building before it exists.

Graduated with a Bachelor of Architecture from Himalaya College of Engineering in 2023, and have been building a practice grounded in site reality — including fault and damage inspection work on an earthquake-affected apartment, which keeps every drawing I make honest about how structures actually behave.
